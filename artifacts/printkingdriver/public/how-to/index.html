How These Guides Are Organised

Each how-to is built around a single task — setting up a driver, sharing a device, scanning to email — and is written so you can complete it without reading anything else first. Where a related task or background concept matters, the guide links to it.

If you need broader background on how devices and drivers work, see our how it works page or the print technology library.

Looking for Something Specific?

If your task is not in the list above, the most likely matches are in common issues (for issues with existing setups) or in the brand-specific pages under brands. Contact us to suggest a guide and we will add it.
